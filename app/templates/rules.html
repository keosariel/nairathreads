Guidelines

In Submissions

– You will not post, discuss, request, or link to anything that violates local or Lagos States law.

– Please submit the original source. If a post reports on something found on another site, submit the latter.

– Please don't delete and repost. Deletion is for things that shouldn't have been submitted in the first place.

– Don't solicit upvotes, comments, or submissions. Users should vote and comment when they run across something they personally find interesting—not for promotion.

– If you submit a video or pdf or image, please warn us by appending [video] or [pdf] or [image] to the title.

In Comments

– Comments should get more thoughtful and substantive, not less, as a topic gets more divisive.

– Please don't use uppercase for emphasis. If you want to emphasize a word or phrase, put *asterisks* around it and it will get italicized.

In General

– The quality of posts is extremely important to this community. Contributors are encouraged to provide high-quality and informative comments. Please refrain from posting the following:

  • Irrelevant catchphrases or copypasta
    Example: "What the fuck did you just fucking say about me, abi this one dey mad ni?..."
  • Indecipherable text
    Example: "lol u tk him 2da bar|?"
  • Ironic shitposting
    Example: "upboads for le funy maymay unbaaaaaaaaaaalievable~~!"
  • Gibberish text
    Example: "hsvjsvisieihsbiohedbewok;f'sa;e"

– Submitting false or misclassified reports, or otherwise abusing the reporting system may result in a ban. Replying to a thread stating that you've reported or "saged" it, or another post, is also not allowed.

– Please don't comment about the voting on comments. It never does any good, and it makes boring reading.

– No spamming or flooding of any kind. No intentionally evading spam or post filters.

– Advertising (all forms) is not welcome—this includes any type of referral linking, "offers", soliciting, begging, stream threads, etc.

– The use of scrapers, bots, or other automated posting or downloading scripts is prohibited. Users may also not post from proxies, VPNs, or Tor exit nodes.
